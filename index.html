About

Hey, I'm Arisha! Hiking in summer, skiing in winter — I'm a woman of many hobbies.

I moved from Russia to Kazakhstan at 19 when the war started. Best decision I ever made, even if the first year was a lot.

I joke in 3 languages and can stand on 1 head. I've been to 25 countries and I'm always planning the next one. At 20 I went to Cannes Lions as a competitor — and in June 2026 I'm going back for my first gold ;)

I do yoga every day, drink Chinese tea, and read Russian classics. I'm also studying philology at uni because apparently one passion is never enough.

I believe in good teamwork, planning, and work-life balance that's actually balanced. I've never burned out — not because I work less, but because I rest properly.

Whether it's a big campaign, a weird idea, or just a coffee chat about design — I'm in.
